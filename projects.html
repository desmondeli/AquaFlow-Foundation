Our Work

Projects & Programs

Six active initiatives bringing clean water, sanitation, and hygiene education to communities across Ghana.

Ashanti • Northern • Volta • Central

All Active Projects

Every project below is developed with the host community from planning through handover, so the impact continues long after construction ends.

Yendi, Northern Region

Borehole for Life

Drilled and equipped a mechanized borehole serving the Yendi community, replacing an unsafe, hours-long water collection routine.

Completed & operational

Impact: 1,200+ residents now have year-round access to clean water.

Techiman, Ashanti Region

Clean Streams Initiative

Rehabilitated a contaminated local water source and built a community-managed filtration station.

Ongoing maintenance

Impact: Waterborne illness reports down significantly since installation.

Ho, Volta Region

Sanitation for Schools

Constructed gender-separated toilet facilities at four primary schools to improve hygiene and attendance.

Completed

Impact: Girls' attendance improved across all four schools.

Kintampo, Central-North Corridor

Rural Wellness Project

Installing hand-dug wells paired with hygiene training across eight surrounding villages.

In progress (60%)

Impact: 5 of 8 villages connected so far.

Cape Coast, Central Region

Community Hygiene Education

Trained households on sanitation practices and waterborne disease prevention through door-to-door outreach.

Completed, results tracked

Impact: 300+ households trained.

Volta Region (multi-district)

AquaFlow Youth Ambassadors

Trains young volunteers to lead water conservation campaigns and peer education in their home communities.

Ongoing, expanding 2027

Impact: 40+ trained youth ambassadors and counting.
